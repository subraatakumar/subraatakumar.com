<li class="d180-md-li">This is a product.</li>

<p class="d180-md-p">Not 60%.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not 80%.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p"><strong>100%.</strong></p>

<p class="d180-md-p">You ask: <em>How will this grow? What environment does it need?</em></p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Architecture is that environment for your product. Features are just clothes.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Products are not built with excitement alone.</p>

<h1 id="the-product-we-are-building" class="d180-md-h1">The Product We Are Building</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are building <strong>Offline Life Archive</strong>.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">A private, offline-first memory vault.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not social.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not cloud-first.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not productivity.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not a journaling gimmick.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Just this:</p>
<blockquote class="d180-md-blockquote">
<p class="d180-md-p">Your private digital life. Stored locally. Owned by you.</p>
</blockquote>
<p class="d180-md-p">Before writing a single line of feature code, we defined:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Who it is for</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">What problem it solves</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">What it will NOT include</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">MVP boundaries</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Emotional anchor</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">Architecture must serve that clarity.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not ego.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Not trends.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="why-architecture-on-day-one" class="d180-md-h1">Why Architecture on Day One?</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">Because:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Architecture determines how fast you move later.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Architecture decides whether refactoring will destroy you.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Architecture defines whether you build features… or systems.</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">Most apps die in month 6 — not because of features, but because of messy foundations.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are building differently.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="the-philosophy-build-small-design-big" class="d180-md-h1">The Philosophy: Build Small. Design Big.</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are building an MVP.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">But we are designing for extraction.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">This app is not just an app.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">It is:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">A privacy product</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">A case study</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">The foundation of something larger (Production OS)</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">So the question today is not:</p>
<blockquote class="d180-md-blockquote">
<p class="d180-md-p">“How do we build the timeline screen?”</p>
</blockquote>
<p class="d180-md-p">It is:</p>
<blockquote class="d180-md-blockquote">
<p class="d180-md-p">“How do we ensure the timeline screen never pollutes our business logic?”</p>
</blockquote>
<p class="d180-md-p">That’s architecture thinking.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="the-5-layer-architecture-model" class="d180-md-h1">The 5-Layer Architecture Model</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are using a strict layered model:</p>

      <div class="relative my-6 code-block">
        <div class="absolute right-3 top-3 flex items-center gap-2 z-50 pointer-events-auto">
          
          <button data-copy class="copy-btn d180-copy-btn">Copy</button>
        </div>
        <pre class="d180-code-pre line-numbers"><code>App Layer
Module Layer
Core Layer
Shared Layer
Data Layer
</code></pre>
      </div>
    
<p class="d180-md-p">Let’s break that down.</p>
<h3 id="app-layer" class="d180-md-h3">App Layer</h3>
<p class="d180-md-p">Navigation and global orchestration only.</p>
<h3 id="module-layer" class="d180-md-h3">Module Layer</h3>
<p class="d180-md-p">Feature-level business logic (archive, tagging, sharing, settings).</p>
<h3 id="core-layer" class="d180-md-h3">Core Layer</h3>
<p class="d180-md-p">Infrastructure:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Encryption</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Secure storage</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Filesystem</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Config</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Devtools</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="shared-layer" class="d180-md-h3">Shared Layer</h3>
<p class="d180-md-p">Reusable, presentational UI components only.</p>
<h3 id="data-layer" class="d180-md-h3">Data Layer</h3>
<p class="d180-md-p">Encrypted Realm database.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Each layer has boundaries.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">No cross-contamination.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">If layers bleed into each other, refactoring becomes inevitable.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="folder-structure-production-ready-from-day-one" class="d180-md-h1">Folder Structure (Production-Ready From Day One)</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">We created a structure that supports growth:</p>

      <div class="relative my-6 code-block">
        <div class="absolute right-3 top-3 flex items-center gap-2 z-50 pointer-events-auto">
          
          <button data-copy class="copy-btn d180-copy-btn">Copy</button>
        </div>
        <pre class="d180-code-pre line-numbers"><code>src/
  app/
  core/
  modules/
  shared/
</code></pre>
      </div>
    
<p class="d180-md-p">This is not over-engineering.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">This is prevention.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Most MVPs collapse because everything lives inside <code class="d180-md-inline-code">screens/</code>.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are separating:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Infrastructure from features</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Business logic from UI</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Storage from services</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Reusable systems from product modules</li>
</ul>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="strict-architectural-rules-non-negotiable" class="d180-md-h1">Strict Architectural Rules (Non-Negotiable)</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">From today onward:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">No business logic inside UI components.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">No direct Realm calls inside screens.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Repository layer handles database access.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Services handle business logic.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Encryption lives only inside <code class="d180-md-inline-code">core/security</code>.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">File handling lives only inside <code class="d180-md-inline-code">core/fileSystem</code>.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Modules never depend directly on other modules.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">No hardcoded theme values in UI.</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">These rules are boring.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">And that’s the point.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Boring architecture scales.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="why-we-are-not-using-redux-yet" class="d180-md-h1">Why We Are NOT Using Redux (Yet)</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">This is important.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are not using:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Redux</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Zustand</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">MobX</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Any global state tool</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">Why?</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Because:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Realm is the single source of truth.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">UI subscribes through custom hooks.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Derived state only.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">No duplicated global state.</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">Premature abstraction is forbidden.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Complexity must be earned.</p>
<hr class="d180-md-hr" />
<h1 id="security-from-day-one" class="d180-md-h1">Security From Day One</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">Most MVPs say:</p>
<blockquote class="d180-md-blockquote">
<p class="d180-md-p">“We’ll add encryption later.”</p>
</blockquote>
<p class="d180-md-p">We won’t.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">From Day 1:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">Realm database is encrypted.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Encryption key is generated per installation.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Key stored securely (Keychain / Keystore).</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">PIN stored as hash (never plain text).</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">App lock is an overlay — not a navigation route.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Encryption and lock are separate layers.</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">Data protection ≠ session protection.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">This is infrastructure, not a feature.</p>

<h1 id="sharing-without-cloud" class="d180-md-h1">Sharing Without Cloud</h1>
<p class="d180-md-p">We are not building servers.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Sharing will be:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">

<li class="d180-md-li">Encrypted bundle</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Password protected</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">OS share sheet</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">No backend.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">No P2P.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">No link sharing.</p>
<p class="d180-md-p">Local-first means local-first.</p>

<p class="d180-md-p">UI must remain replaceable.</p>

<h1 id="why-navigation-matters" class="d180-md-h1">Why Navigation Matters</h1>

<p class="d180-md-p">Without navigation:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">screens cannot connect</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">users cannot move between features</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">app flow cannot exist</li>

<p class="d180-md-p">Navigation defines:</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">how screens transition</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">how parameters move between screens</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">how back navigation works</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">how the app structure evolves</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">In a well-architected app, navigation should be <strong>predictable and centralized</strong>.</p>

<h1 id="creating-type-safe-navigation" class="d180-md-h1">Creating Type-Safe Navigation</h1>

<p class="d180-md-p">If we misspell it:</p>

      <div class="relative my-6 code-block">

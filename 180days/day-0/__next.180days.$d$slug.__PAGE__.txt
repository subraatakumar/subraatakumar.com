<p class="d180-md-p">I’m building a real privacy-first app called Offline Life Archive from scratch.</p>

<p class="d180-md-p">Today, I’m starting this as a 180-day mentorship journey.</p>
<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">One focused hour daily.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Clear architecture.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Security from Day 1.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">Production discipline.</li>
</ul>
<p class="d180-md-p">And along the way, I’ll be joined by Maya 🤖 — an AI-generated junior developer who will question my decisions.</p>

<ul class="d180-md-ul">
<li class="d180-md-li">AI can write syntax.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">It cannot design systems.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">It cannot own trade-offs.</li>
<li class="d180-md-li">It cannot think in layers.</li>

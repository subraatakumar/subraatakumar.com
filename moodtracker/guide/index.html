MoodTracker Setup and Usage Guide

This guide mirrors the real MoodTracker app flow: setup reminders, create mood labels, log check-ins, monitor patterns, and secure/backup your data.

Guide Sections

Start From Home Screen

Open the app and begin with the home screen. It shows your latest mood activity and quick actions.

1Open Home and review today's mood timeline.
2Tap quick add to log your current mood.
3Add a short note if you want more context.

Create A Mood Entry

Use the mood entry flow to capture how you feel with optional tags and notes.

1Choose your mood label from presets or custom labels.
2Set date/time if recording a past moment.
3Add optional note and save the entry.

Set Reminder Schedule

Configure check-in reminders so the app supports your daily emotional awareness routine.

1Add reminder times aligned with your day.
2Set follow-up reminders for missed check-ins.
3Choose notification style: silent or sound.

Manage Mood Labels

Customize your mood labels so logging feels personal and meaningful.

1Open Manage Labels from settings.
2Add, edit, or reorder mood labels.
3Assign colors to improve visual scanning.

Review Mood Trends

Use trend charts to understand emotional patterns over time.

1Open the Trends view from Home.
2Choose weekly or monthly range.
3Look for repeating triggers and recovery patterns.

Backup and Restore Data

Use backup and restore when changing devices so your mood history stays safe.

1Open Backup & Restore screen from configuration.
2Create backup and confirm latest timestamp.
3Restore only from trusted backup points.

Personalize Appearance

Choose theme and appearance options to make the app feel natural for long-term use.

1Open Appearance settings from Home menu.
2Preview available themes and pick one that fits your style.
3Apply appearance and continue tracking with same data.

Protect Data with Privacy Controls

MoodTracker follows local-first design. Add lock controls for extra protection when sharing your device.

1Enable PIN lock from configuration.
2Enable biometric unlock after PIN is configured.
3Review privacy policy and terms for the complete data model.

Quick FAQ

Do I need an account to use MoodTracker?

No. Core tracking flow is designed to work without account signup.

Can I customize mood labels?

Yes. Manage Labels lets you add your own mood labels with color and ordering controls.

Can I add notes to each mood check-in?

Yes. Entries support optional notes so you can capture context around how you felt.

Where should I start if I am new?

Start with reminder setup, then your label list, then daily check-ins. After that, review trends weekly.

For the next 180 days, I’m starting something intentional.

I’m building a real privacy-first app called Offline Life Archive from scratch.

- Not a tutorial project.
- Not a clone.
- Not a demo.

A production-ready product — with the goal of shipping it to the App Store and Play Store.

But this journey is not just about building an app. It’s about becoming product-minded in the AI era.

Today, I’m starting this as a 180-day mentorship journey.

- One focused hour daily.
- Clear architecture.
- Security from Day 1.
- Production discipline.

And along the way, I’ll be joined by Maya 🤖 — an AI-generated junior developer who will question my decisions.

![](/images/day0.png)

Because in a world where AI can generate code, the real value of a developer is judgment.

- AI can write syntax.
- It cannot design systems.
- It cannot own trade-offs.
- It cannot think in layers.

Day 0 is about commitment.

Day 1 starts with architecture before features.

If you're a developer who wants to evolve beyond just coding — you're welcome to follow this journey.

## 🛑 What We Did NOT Use AI For

Very important.

We did NOT:

- Auto-generate code blindly
- Let AI decide architecture
- Add features because AI suggested them
- Skip thinking because “AI knows”

AI is a junior assistant.

Not a CTO.
